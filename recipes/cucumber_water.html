Cucumber water

Description

Ingredients

  • Water
  • 10 cm length of cucumber
  • Mint

Steps

  1. Cut the cucumber in baton or any other shape you like.
  2. Cut the mint tiny.
  3. Put the cucumber and mint in a jar.
  4. Add water.
  5. Put in the fridge.

You can refill with water as long as the cuncumber taste remain and the parts are in good shape.
